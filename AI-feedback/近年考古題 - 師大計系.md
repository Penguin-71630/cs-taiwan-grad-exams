Without forwarding, RAW hazard requires stalls until WB completes. `add $1,$2,$3` produces $1 (available after WB, cycle 5). `sub $4,$1,$5` needs $1 at ID (cycle 3) — 2 stalls. `add $6,$1,$7` needs $1 — by then available. `add $8,$4,$1` needs $4 (produced by sub). Count all stalls for each dependency. Total cycles = 5 + (instructions - 1) + total stalls.
- Key concept: pipeline stalls without forwarding — 2-cycle penalty per RAW dependency.
