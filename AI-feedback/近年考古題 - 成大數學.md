$a_{n+2} - 4a_{n+1} + 3a_n = -200$. Homogeneous: $r^2 - 4r + 3 = 0$, roots $r = 1, 3$. Particular: $a_n^{(p)} = c$ (constant). $c - 4c + 3c = -200$, $0 = -200$ — contradiction. ### Problem 8
